Dining

At Southeast Pearl Hotel & Resort, you can sample, delicious,
authentic local and international cuisine prepared using the freshest of ingredients.
Enjoy all-day dining at the Café – Southeast Pearl Hotel main dining space or sit back and relax while sipping a newly discovered drink at the Lounge.

The Cafe

This bustling 315-seater interactive show-kitchen buffet offers a wide range of crowd favorites.
Serving breakfast, The Café showcases vibrant cooking and buffet stations with engaging displays of fresh produce, delicacies, and tasty drinks. Each counter spotlights menus from all over the world for a true continental breakfast experience.​


Hours
Break fast

Buffet service schedule

The Lounge

Adjacent to the Lobby is the Lounge, a comfortable, stylish spot to meet friends and colleagues, or simply relax and unwind throughout the day.
Whether settled into a plush chair or people-watching at the bar, you can enjoy well-crafted cocktails, premium wine, tea and coffee, as well as an international snack menu.​


Hours
Friday through Saturday

10:00 AM - 6:00 PM

The Peak

A beautiful collision of a grill restaurant, speakeasy, whisky bar, and a music lounge, The Peak will take you from dining, to drinks, to an exceptional experience of the high life.​
